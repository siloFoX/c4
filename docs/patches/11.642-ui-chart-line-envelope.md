## Summary

New `ChartLineEnvelope` UI primitive at
`web/src/components/ui/chart-line-envelope.tsx` -- a pure-SVG
line chart with a moving average envelope of fixed percentage
bands above and below.

## Indicator math

A moving average envelope brackets a moving average with two
bands at a fixed percentage offset.

- `computeLineEnvelopeBasis` -- the `period`-bar simple moving
  average (the basis / middle line).
- `computeLineEnvelopeBands` -- the envelope edges:

```
upper = basis * (1 + percent/100)
lower = basis * (1 - percent/100)
```

Because the offset is a fixed percentage *of the basis*, it
scales with the basis -- the envelope keeps a constant relative
width regardless of volatility, and even a flat series carries a
band. Both series are null through the `period - 1` warm-up.

### Worked fixture

`period 2`, `percent 25`, price series `[6, 18, 10, 30, 14]`.
With a two-bar window the basis is the midpoint, and a 25
percent envelope multiplies it by 1.25 and 0.75, so every value
lands on a clean number:

```
[6,18]  -> basis 12, upper 15,   lower 9
[18,10] -> basis 14, upper 17.5, lower 10.5
[10,30] -> basis 20, upper 25,   lower 15
[30,14] -> basis 22, upper 27.5, lower 16.5
```

The price runs above the basis on 2 bars and below on 2.

## API

- `getLineEnvelopeFinitePoints(points)` -- drop points with a
  non-finite `x` or `value`.
- `normalizeLineEnvelopePeriod(period, fallback)` -- coerce to a
  positive integer.
- `normalizeLineEnvelopePercent(percent, fallback)` -- coerce to
  a positive finite number.
- `computeLineEnvelopeBasis(values, period)` -- the moving
  average basis, null through the warm-up.
- `computeLineEnvelopeBands(values, period, percent)` -- the
  `{ upper, lower }` envelope series, null through the warm-up.
- `runLineEnvelope(points, options?)` -- sort finite points by
  `x`, compute the basis and envelope series, return
  `{ series, period, percent, basis, upper, lower, samples,
  basisFinal, upperFinal, lowerFinal, aboveCount, belowCount,
  ok }`. Each sample is classified `above` / `below` / `on` the
  basis. `ok` is false for fewer than two points.
- `computeLineEnvelopeLayout(options)` -- project the price,
  basis, upper and lower paths plus a filled band area.
- `describeLineEnvelopeChart(data, options?)` -- ARIA
  description.

## Component

`ChartLineEnvelope` is a `forwardRef` region (`role="region"`)
with an ARIA-linked description, a `role="img"` SVG, axis ticks
and grid, a config badge showing the period and percent, a
three-series legend (Price / Basis / Envelope) with toggle, a
hover/focus tooltip exposing the value, basis, upper, lower and
position, and a `motion-safe:animate-fade-in` entrance. It
consumes `{ x, value }` points. The envelope edges are drawn as
upper and lower lines with a translucent filled area between
them; the basis is a separate line with per-bar markers. Every
node carries a `data-section` attribute.

## Distinctness

- **vs chart-line-stddev** -- the standard deviation band sets
  its offset from the *rolling dispersion* of the price, so the
  band widens and narrows with volatility. The envelope offset
  is a *constant percentage* of the basis -- its relative width
  never changes.
- **vs chart-line-bandwidth** -- bandwidth reduces a band to a
  single scalar in a sub-panel. The envelope draws the full band
  on the price panel.
- **vs the smoothing overlays** -- those draw a single smoothed
  line. The envelope draws a channel: a basis line bracketed by
  an upper and a lower edge.
