## Indicator

Manning Stoller's STARC Bands (Stoller Average Range Channels)
wrap a short moving average in average-true-range bands. On a
close-only series the true range is the close-to-close change,
matching the existing `chart-line-atr` convention, so
`ChartLineStarcPoint` carries `{x, value}`.

- `computeLineStarcSma(closes, period)` -- the short simple
  moving average of the close: the STARC middle band.
- `computeLineStarcAtr(closes, period)` -- Welles Wilder's
  Average True Range over the close-to-close true range.
- `computeLineStarc(closes, maPeriod, atrPeriod, multiplier)` --
  the STARC Bands `{ middle, upper, lower }`: the upper and lower
  bands sit `multiplier` average true ranges above and below the
  moving average.
- `runLineStarc` -- sorts the finite points by x, runs the
  pipeline, and returns per-bar samples classified `above` /
  `below` / `inside` the channel, the zone counts and the final
  band readings; `ok` is false for fewer than two points.
- `computeLineStarcLayout` -- overlays the price line, the three
  band lines and a filled channel area on a single panel, with
  one marker per classified bar.
- `describeLineStarcChart` -- an off-screen sentence summary for
  assistive technology.

## Testability

The STARC pipeline chains a recursive Wilder ATR with a simple
moving average. The fixture sidesteps the recursion: every close
steps by exactly +/-6, so every close-to-close true range is a
constant 6 and Wilder's ATR -- a weighted blend of a constant --
settles at exactly 6. Every three-bar window sums to a multiple
of three so the moving average is an exact integer, and with the
multiplier and ATR both clean the bands land on exact values,
asserted with `toEqual`. The closes walk up and down so the close
pierces above the channel, dips below it and stays inside, making
all three zones appear. A separate dyadic fixture checks the
Wilder recurrence after the seed.
