## Summary

New `ChartLineWeightedClose` UI primitive in
`web/src/components/ui/chart-line-weighted-close.tsx` -- a pure-SVG
single-panel line chart with a **Weighted Close** overlay averaging the bar
with a double-weighted close. The close line and the Weighted Close line
share one panel.

## The computation

The Weighted Close averages each bar's high, low and a double-weighted
close -- `(high + low + 2 * close) / 4`. Giving the close half the total
weight (2 of 4) pulls the result toward the close, so the Weighted Close
hugs the close more tightly than the equal-thirds Typical Price does. It
works on an OHLC-like bar, so `ChartLineWeightedClosePoint` carries
`{x, high, low, close}`.

- `computeLineWeightedClose(bars)` -- the Weighted Close per bar. It is
  purely per-bar with no lookback, so it is defined for every bar; a bar
  with a non-finite high, low or close yields null.

Each bar is classified by where the Weighted Close sits relative to the
close: **above** (the close finished in the lower part of its range),
**below** (the close finished strong, in the upper part) or **equal**.

## Worked fixture

The Weighted Close is `(high + low + 2 * close) / 4`, an exact integer
whenever `high`, `low` and `close` are all multiples of 4. The fixture uses
8 OHLC bars built that way:

```
bar      high low close  weighted  zone
1         16   8   12      12      equal
2         20  12   20      18      below
3         28  12   12      16      above
4         24  16   20      20      equal
5         32  24   32      30      below
6         36  16   16      21      above
7         40  32   40      38      below
8         44  20   24      28      above
```

The Weighted Close series `[12,18,16,20,30,21,38,28]` is asserted with
`toEqual`. The close sits level with the Weighted Close (bars 1, 4), below
it (bars 3, 6, 8) and above it (bars 2, 5, 7), so all three zones are
exercised: 3 above, 3 below, 2 equal; final Weighted Close 28, final close
24.

## API

`data` (a `ChartLineWeightedClosePoint[]`), sizing/format/a11y props,
`strokeWidth` / `dotRadius`, the colour props, the toggle flags (`showAxis`
/ `showGrid` / `showDots` default false / `showWeighted` / `showMarkers` /
`showTooltip` / `showConfigBadge` / `showLegend` / `animate`), controlled +
uncontrolled `hiddenSeries` (keys `close` / `weighted`), `formatValue` /
`formatX`, `onPointClick` / `onSeriesToggle`. The Weighted Close is
parameter-free, so there is no period prop.

Exported helpers: `getLineWeightedCloseFinitePoints`,
`computeLineWeightedClose`, `runLineWeightedClose`,
`computeLineWeightedCloseLayout`, `describeLineWeightedCloseChart`, plus the
`DEFAULT_CHART_LINE_WEIGHTED_CLOSE_*` constants and the
`ChartLineWeightedCloseZone` type. Every exported name carries a
`LineWeightedClose` infix so the `export *` barrel line cannot collide.

## Accessibility

Root `<div role="region">` with `aria-label` + `aria-describedby`; SVG
`role="img"`; an off-screen
`data-section="chart-line-weighted-close-aria-desc"` description span; the
close line, the Weighted Close line and every dot / marker are
`role="graphics-symbol" tabIndex={0}`. Every node carries a `data-section`;
the root mirrors `data-total-points`, `data-weighted-final`,
`data-close-final`, `data-above-count`, `data-below-count`,
`data-equal-count` and `data-animate`.
