## Summary

`web/src/components/ui/chart-line-aroon-down.tsx` ships
`<ChartLineAroonDown>`, a pure-SVG two-panel chart with an **Aroon
Down** panel from the distance to the most recent low scaled to the
lookback window. The top panel plots the bar low; the bottom panel
plots the Aroon Down reading in a fixed `[0, 100]` band with dashed
horizontal reference lines at the high and low thresholds.

## Computation

For each bar `i`, the window of `period + 1` bars `[i - period .. i]`
is scanned for the lowest low. The most-recent bar wins on a tie
(strict `<`). The Aroon Down scales the bars-since-the-low to a
percent:

```
barsSinceLow = i - argmin(low over [i - period .. i])
aroonDown    = 100 * (period - barsSinceLow) / period
```

A new low pushes Aroon Down to 100; a low made `period` bars ago
drops it to 0. The reading is bounded to `[0, 100]`. Conventional
reference lines sit at 70 (strong downtrend) and 30 (weak).

## Worked anchors

Pure rational arithmetic, no transcendentals -- integer fixtures
compute to exact integer values. At period 4 every reading is a
multiple of 25.

- **Rising series** -- lows `[1..10]`. At every defined bar the
  LOWEST low is the OLDEST bar in the window, so
  `barsSinceLow = period` and `aroonDown = 0`. Asserted with
  `toBe(0)`.
- **Falling series** -- lows `[10..1]`. Every bar makes a new low,
  so `barsSinceLow = 0` and `aroonDown = 100`. Asserted with
  `toBe(100)`.
- **Constant series** -- lows `[5 x 10]`. Every bar ties; the
  most-recent-tie rule (strict `<` keeps the earliest scan
  position) pins `barsSinceLow = 0` -> `aroonDown = 100`.
- **Worked valley fixture** -- lows
  `[6, 5, 4, 3, 2, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5]` at period 4. The low bottoms
  at bar 5 (value 1); the window scan from `i = 5` onward locks
  the low at bar 5 while `i` advances. Step by step: `bar 4 ->
  aroonDown=100` (lowest=2 at idx 4), `bar 5 -> 100` (lowest=1 at
  idx 5), `bar 6 -> 75` (barsSince=1), `bar 7 -> 50`, `bar 8 ->
  25`, `bar 9 -> 0`. Asserted with `toEqual([null x4, 100, 100,
  75, 50, 25, 0])`.
- **`[0, 100]` bound** -- every defined value on a wave fixture
  is in the bound.

## Distinct from neighbouring primitives

- vs `<ChartLineAroonUp>` -- the Aroon Up is the mirror primitive
  scanning for the HIGHEST high; this primitive scans for the
  LOWEST low. Same `[0, 100]` band and threshold conventions, but
  the meaning of "strong" flips: a high Aroon Down reading means
  a fresh low (strong DOWNTREND), not a fresh high.
- vs `<ChartLineAroonOsc>` -- the Aroon Oscillator combines the
  two as `Aroon Up - Aroon Down` in `[-100, 100]`; this primitive
  isolates the Aroon Down half.
- vs `<ChartLineRsi>` -- the RSI measures bar-to-bar price
  changes; Aroon Down measures the RECENCY of the lowest low.

## API

`data` -- a single `ChartLineAroonDownPoint[]` (`{x, low}`).
`period` (default 25, integer >= 1). `thresholdHigh` (default 70,
in `[0, 100]`). `thresholdLow` (default 30, in `[0, 100]`).
Standard sizing / colour / a11y props plus `pricePanelRatio`
(default 0.55, clamped to `[0.3, 0.8]`), `gap`, `strokeWidth`,
`dotRadius`, `tickCount`. Toggle flags: `showAxis`, `showGrid`,
`showDots` (default false), `showAroonDown`, `showThresholdLines`,
`showMarkers`, `showTooltip`, `showConfigBadge`, `showLegend`,
`animate`. Controlled and uncontrolled visibility via
`hiddenSeries` (`'price' | 'aroonDown'`). Callbacks:
`onPointClick({point})`, `onSeriesToggle({seriesId, hidden})`.
Format functions: `formatValue`, `formatX`. ARIA: `ariaLabel`,
`ariaDescription`.

## Pure helpers

- `DEFAULT_CHART_LINE_AROON_DOWN_*` -- defaults (period 25,
  thresholds 70 / 30, etc.).
- `CHART_LINE_AROON_DOWN_MIN/MAX` -- 0 and 100 (the panel bounds).
- `getLineAroonDownFinitePoints` -- keep only points with finite x
  and low.
- `normalizeLineAroonDownPeriod(period, fallback)` -- integer >= 1;
  else fallback.
- `normalizeLineAroonDownThreshold(threshold, fallback)` -- finite
  in `[0, 100]`; else fallback.
- `computeLineAroonDown(bars, period)` -- per-bar Aroon Down;
  warm-up null; the most-recent bar wins on a tie.
- `classifyLineAroonDownZone(value, thresholdHigh, thresholdLow)`
  -- `'strong' | 'mid' | 'weak' | 'none'`.
- `runLineAroonDown(points, options?)` --
  `{series, period, thresholdHigh, thresholdLow, aroonDown,
  samples, aroonDownFinal, strongCount, midCount, weakCount, ok}`.
- `computeLineAroonDownLayout({data, period, thresholdHigh,
  thresholdLow, ...})` -- two panels with the low line on top,
  the Aroon Down line below in a fixed `[-5, 105]` band, one
  marker per defined-aroon bar coloured by zone, plus
  `thresholdHighY` and `thresholdLowY` for the reference lines.
- `describeLineAroonDownChart(data, options?)` -- ARIA summary
  mentioning the period, the threshold band, the new-low rule and
  the zone counts.

## a11y / data hooks

Root `<div role="region">` with `aria-label` + `aria-describedby`; the
SVG is `role="img"` with the same label; an sr-only
`data-section="chart-line-aroon-down-aria-desc"` span carries the
description. The low line, every price dot, the Aroon Down line and
every marker carry `role="graphics-symbol"` and `tabIndex={0}`.
Every node carries a `data-section`. Root mirrors `data-period`,
`data-threshold-high`, `data-threshold-low`, `data-aroon-down-final`,
`data-strong-count`, `data-mid-count`, `data-weak-count`,
`data-total-points`, `data-animate`, `data-empty`. Grid / axis /
panel-label nodes carry `data-panel="price"|"aroon"`. Threshold
lines carry `data-direction="high"|"low"`. Markers expose
`data-zone` and `data-aroon-down`.
