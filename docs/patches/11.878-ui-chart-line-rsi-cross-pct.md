## Summary

Added `<ChartLineRsiCrossPct />` -- a pure-SVG, dual-panel
React/TS primitive that renders the close in the top panel and
overlays the Wilder RSI, its EMA-smoothed signal line, and the
`rsiPct = (RSI - signal) / 100 * 100` deviation channel in the
bottom panel. The percent treatment surfaces the *relative
momentum strength* (how stretched RSI is above or below its
signal, as a percent of the 0..100 RSI scale) separate from the
sign-based RSI-over-signal crossings rendered by 11.871
`chart-line-rsi-cross-sig`:

```
gain[i]   = max(close[i] - close[i-1], 0)
loss[i]   = max(close[i-1] - close[i], 0)
avgG[i]   = Wilder smooth of gains, length
avgL[i]   = Wilder smooth of losses, length
RSI[i]    = avgL === 0
              ? (avgG === 0 ? 50 : 100)
              : 100 - 100 / (1 + avgG / avgL)
signal[i] = EMA(RSI, signalLength)
rsiPct[i] = (RSI[i] - signal[i]) / 100 * 100
```

Defaults: `length = 14` (canonical Wilder), `signalLength = 9`.
Regime classifier: `above` (rsiPct > 0), `below` (< 0),
`at` (= 0), `none` (null).

## Bit-exact anchors (three)

- **CONST close = K**: every gain = loss = 0 -> RSI = 50 every
  bar. Signal EMA of 50s = 50. rsiPct = (50 - 50) / 100 * 100
  = 0. Regime `at` everywhere after the chained warmup.
- **LINEAR UP step > 0**: gains > 0 and losses = 0 every bar
  -> RSI = 100 every bar. Signal EMA of 100s = 100. rsiPct = 0.
- **LINEAR DOWN step < 0**: gains = 0 and losses > 0 every bar
  -> RSI = 0 every bar. Signal EMA of 0s = 0. rsiPct = 0.

All three anchors hit `rsiPct = 0` bit-exact and are verified
across multiple K / start tuples in the integration block.

## Difference vs other RSI primitives

| primitive | bottom panel | use-case |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `chart-line-rsi-cross-sig` (11.871) | RSI + signal + cross dots | trigger events |
| `chart-line-rsi-cross-pct` (11.878) | RSI + signal + (RSI - signal) magnitude | momentum strength magnitude separate from crossings |

The cross-pct variant surfaces the *magnitude* of RSI's stretch
above or below its signal as a continuous channel (scaled by the
0..100 RSI range), distinct from the discrete sign-based
crossover events 11.871 emits.
