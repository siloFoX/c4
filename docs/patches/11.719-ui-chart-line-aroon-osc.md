## Summary

`web/src/components/ui/chart-line-aroon-osc.tsx` ships
`<ChartLineAroonOsc>`, a pure-SVG two-panel chart with the **Aroon
Oscillator** -- the spread between Aroon Up and Aroon Down. The top
panel plots the bar midpoint; the bottom panel plots the oscillator
inside a fixed [-100, 100] band with a zero line and one marker per
bar coloured by the sign of the reading.

## Computation

The Aroon Oscillator reads the **recency gap** between the highest
high and the lowest low in the recent lookback. For each bar `i`, the
window of `period + 1` bars `[i - period .. i]` is scanned for the
highest high and the lowest low; the most-recent bar wins on a tie.
`barsSinceHigh` and `barsSinceLow` are the distances from `i` to those
extremes, each in `[0, period]`. Aroon Up scales the bars-since to a
percent:

```
aroonUp   = 100 * (period - barsSinceHigh) / period
aroonDown = 100 * (period - barsSinceLow)  / period
osc       = aroonUp - aroonDown
```

A new high pushes Aroon Up to 100; a high made `period` bars ago drops
it to 0. The oscillator is therefore bounded to `[-100, 100]`: a fresh
high with an old low reads +100 (the bulls have the recency), the
mirror reads -100 (the bears do), 0 is balanced.

## Worked anchors

The pipeline is pure rational arithmetic, so integer fixtures compute
to exact integer Aroon values. At period 4 every Aroon reading is a
multiple of 25; their difference is therefore a multiple of 25 in
`[-100, 100]`.

- **UP_RAMP** -- closes `1..10`, high = low = close, period 4. Every
  defined bar has barsSinceHigh = 0 (the current bar) and barsSinceLow
  = 4 (the oldest in the window). Osc = `100 - 0 = 100` -- asserted
  with `toEqual([null x4, 100, 100, 100, 100, 100, 100])`.
- **DOWN_RAMP** -- closes `10..1`. Mirror of the above: barsSinceHigh
  = 4, barsSinceLow = 0. Osc = `0 - 100 = -100` -- asserted with
  `toEqual([null x4, -100, -100, -100, -100, -100, -100])`.
- **CONST** -- closes `50 x10`. Every bar ties for both the high and
  the low; the most-recent-tie convention pins both barsSince to 0,
  giving Aroon Up = Aroon Down = 100 and Osc = 0 -- asserted with
  `toEqual([null x4, 0, 0, 0, 0, 0, 0])`.
- **MIXED_PEAK** -- closes `[1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2]` (a peak at
  bar 5). At period 4 the worked oscillator series is
  `[null x4, 100, 100, 75, -50, -75, -100]`: the rising leg keeps
  pinning Aroon Up to 100 while Aroon Down stays at 0 (osc 100), then
  the peak ages out and Aroon Up decays in 25-point steps while Aroon
  Down jumps to 100, flipping the oscillator negative.

## Distinct from neighbouring primitives

- vs `<ChartLineRsi>` -- the RSI is `100 - 100 / (1 + sumUp / sumDown)`
  over the bar-to-bar **price changes**; the Aroon Oscillator is the
  difference of two recency percents over the **highs and lows**. The
  RSI is unsigned in `[0, 100]`; the Aroon Osc is signed in
  `[-100, 100]`.
- vs `<ChartLineStochastic>` -- the Stochastic ranks the current close
  inside the lookback's range; the Aroon Osc ranks the **time** of the
  high vs the **time** of the low. The Stochastic responds to price
  level, the Aroon Osc to the recency of the extremes.
- vs `<ChartLineTrendTrigger>` -- the TTF compares the buying power
  (recent high minus prior low) against the selling power (prior high
  minus recent low) and scales to `[-100, 100]` via the percent rule
  `100 * (BP - SP) / ((BP + SP) / 2)`. The Aroon Osc is the cleaner
  time-only cousin: it ignores the magnitude of the extremes and uses
  only how long ago they happened.
- vs `<ChartLineUdRatio>` -- the UD Ratio is the bull share of the
  total bar-to-bar change over the lookback in `[0, 100]`; the Aroon
  Osc is the time-recency spread of the high vs the low in
  `[-100, 100]`.

## API

`data` -- a single `ChartLineAroonOscPoint[]` (`{x, high, low}`).
`period` -- the lookback (default 25, coerced to an integer >= 1).
Standard sizing / colour / a11y props plus `pricePanelRatio` (default
0.55, clamped to `[0.3, 0.8]`), `gap`, `strokeWidth`, `dotRadius`,
`tickCount`. Toggle flags: `showAxis`, `showGrid`, `showDots` (default
false), `showOsc`, `showZeroLine`, `showMarkers`, `showTooltip`,
`showConfigBadge`, `showLegend`, `animate`. Controlled and
uncontrolled visibility via `hiddenSeries` (`'price' | 'osc'`).
Callbacks: `onPointClick({point})`, `onSeriesToggle({seriesId, hidden})`.
Format functions: `formatValue`, `formatX`. ARIA: `ariaLabel`,
`ariaDescription`.

## Pure helpers

- `DEFAULT_CHART_LINE_AROON_OSC_*` -- default sizes, colours, period,
  panel ratio.
- `CHART_LINE_AROON_OSC_BOUND` -- 100 (the magnitude bound).
- `getLineAroonOscFinitePoints` -- keep only bars with a finite
  `x` / `high` / `low`.
- `normalizeLineAroonOscPeriod(period, fallback)` -- integer >= 1
  with floor, otherwise fallback.
- `computeLineAroonOscAroonUp(bars, period)` -- per-bar Aroon Up.
- `computeLineAroonOscAroonDown(bars, period)` -- per-bar Aroon Down.
- `computeLineAroonOsc(bars, period)` --
  `{aroonUp, aroonDown, osc}`.
- `classifyLineAroonOscZone(osc)` -- `'up' | 'down' | 'flat' | 'none'`.
- `runLineAroonOsc(points, options?)` --
  `{series, period, aroonUp, aroonDown, osc, samples, oscFinal,
  upCount, downCount, flatCount, ok}`.
- `computeLineAroonOscLayout({data, period, ...})` -- stacks the
  midpoint panel above the oscillator panel (the oscillator panel
  fixed to `[-110, 110]` so the full `[-100, 100]` band always shows
  with headroom), projects `pricePath`, `priceDots`, `oscPath`,
  `markers` (one entry per finite-osc bar coloured by zone), and
  `zeroY` for the zero line. Degenerate canvas / fewer than 2 points
  -> `ok = false`.
- `describeLineAroonOscChart(data, options?)` -- ARIA summary
  mentioning the indicator name, Aroon Up + Aroon Down, the lookback
  and the zone counts.

## a11y / data hooks

Root `<div role="region">` with `aria-label` + `aria-describedby`; the
SVG is `role="img"` with the same label; an sr-only
`data-section="chart-line-aroon-osc-aria-desc"` span carries the
description. The midpoint line, the oscillator line, every price dot
and every marker carry `role="graphics-symbol"` and `tabIndex={0}`.
Every node carries a `data-section` (chart-line-aroon-osc /
-aria-desc / -svg / -empty / -grid / -grid-line / -axes / -axis /
-tick-label / -panel-label / -zero-line / -price-path / -dots / -dot
/ -osc-line / -markers / -marker / -tooltip / -tooltip-x /
-tooltip-high / -tooltip-low / -tooltip-aroon / -tooltip-osc /
-tooltip-zone / -legend / -legend-item / -legend-swatch /
-legend-label / -legend-stats / -badge / -badge-icon / -badge-config).
Root mirrors `data-period`, `data-osc-final`, `data-up-count`,
`data-down-count`, `data-flat-count`, `data-total-points`,
`data-animate`, `data-empty`. Markers expose `data-osc` and
`data-zone`.
