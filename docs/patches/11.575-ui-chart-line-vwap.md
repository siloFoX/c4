# 11.575 -- web/src/components/ui/chart-line-vwap.tsx

Pure-SVG line chart with a **volume-weighted average price
(VWAP)** overlay. Unlike every other line primitive, the
data carries two paired fields per period -- `price` and
`volume` -- and the chart plots the cumulative VWAP line on
top of the price line, with the volume drawn as faint bars
along the bottom.

## The VWAP computation

`runLineVwap(points)` walks the series sorted by x and, at
each period, computes the cumulative VWAP:

```
VWAP[t] = sum(price[i] * volume[i]) / sum(volume[i])   for i = 0..t
```

It is the running average price weighted by traded volume,
so a high-volume period pulls the line harder than a
low-volume one. Two edge cases are handled deterministically:

- A **negative volume** is clamped to 0 -- a trade cannot
  have negative size.
- A period whose **cumulative volume is still 0** has a
  `null` VWAP (the ratio is undefined). The VWAP line begins
  only once volume has accumulated.

Each sample also carries `deviation = price - vwap` -- how
far the price trades from its volume-weighted benchmark.

The component fixture makes the math exact: prices
`[10, 20, 30, 20]` with volumes `[100, 100, 200, 100]` give
a cumulative VWAP of `[10, 15, 22.5, 22]`, deviations of
`[0, 5, 7.5, -2]`, a total volume of 500 and a final VWAP of
22.

## Distinct from neighbouring primitives

VWAP is the only line primitive that consumes a paired
`{price, volume}` series -- the volume weighting cannot be
reproduced from a plain `{x, value}` series.
`chart-line-moving-avg` averages by *count* (an unweighted
SMA); `chart-line-ewma` weights by *recency*;
`chart-line-cumulative` sums values. None weight by a second
quantity. The volume bars at the bottom surface the weights
that drive the line.

All exported helpers carry a `LineVwap` infix so the
`export *` barrel line cannot collide.

## Public surface

- `<ChartLineVwap data={...} />` -- a single
  `ChartLineVwapPoint[]` (`{x, price, volume}`).
- `volumeBandRatio` -- the fraction of the panel height the
  volume bars occupy (default 0.22; clamped to [0.05, 0.5]).
- Toggle flags: `showAxis`, `showGrid`, `showDots` (default
  false), `showVwap`, `showVolume`, `showTooltip`,
  `showConfigBadge`, `showLegend`, `animate`.
- Controlled + uncontrolled visibility (`hiddenSeries` keys
  `price` / `vwap` / `volume`).
- Standard sizing + format + a11y props. `formatValue` /
  `formatX`.
- `onPointClick({point})` / `onSeriesToggle({seriesId,
  hidden})`.

## Pure helpers

- `getLineVwapFinitePoints(points)` -- keeps points whose x,
  price and volume are all finite.
- `runLineVwap(points)` -- the cumulative VWAP; reports the
  per-period samples, the total volume and the final VWAP.
- `computeLineVwapLayout({...})` -- projects the price line,
  the VWAP overlay and the volume bars.
- `describeLineVwapChart(data, options?)`.

## ARIA + test selectors

- Root `role="region"` + `aria-label` + `aria-describedby`.
- SVG `role="img"`.
- The price line and every price dot `role="graphics-symbol"`
  + `tabIndex={0}`.
- Every node carries `data-section="chart-line-vwap-..."`.
- Root mirrors `data-total-volume`, `data-vwap-final`,
  `data-total-points`, `data-animate`.
- Volume bars expose `data-volume`. Price dots expose
  `data-price`, `data-volume`, `data-vwap`, `data-deviation`.

## Tooltip / badge / legend

Hovering a price dot shows x, price, volume, the VWAP and
the deviation from it. Config badge: `VWAP final=<vwapFinal>
vol=<totalVolume>`. The legend has three toggle items
(Price / VWAP / Volume) plus the final VWAP.
