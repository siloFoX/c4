## Summary

Added `<ChartLineDemaCrossPct />` -- a pure-SVG, dual-panel
React/TS primitive that overlays the close with the Double
Exponential Moving Average (DEMA) in the top panel and renders
the `close - DEMA` deviation scaled to the close as a percent in
the bottom panel. The percent normalisation makes the lag-
corrected DEMA momentum comparable across instruments at
different price magnitudes:

```
ema1[i]    = EMA(close, length)
ema2[i]    = EMA(ema1,  length)
DEMA[i]    = 2 * ema1[i] - ema2[i]
demaPct[i] = close[i] === 0 ? null
                            : (close[i] - DEMA[i]) / close[i] * 100
```

Regime classifier: `above` (demaPct > 0), `below` (< 0),
`at` (= 0), `none` (null). Defaults: `length = 14`.

## Bit-exact anchor

- **CONST close = K (K > 0)**: both EMAs in the chain collapse to
  K via the SMA-seeded `min === max` precision fix in
  `applyLineDemaCrossPctEma`. `DEMA = 2K - K = K`, deviation = 0,
  `demaPct = 0 / K * 100 = 0`. Regime `at` everywhere after the
  full chained warmup at `2 * length - 2` (ema2 needs another
  full window after ema1 has its first non-null value). Verified
  across multiple K and length tuples. K = 0 triggers the divide-
  by-zero guard -> demaPct = null.

## Difference vs chart-line-tema-cross-pct (11.872)

| primitive | smoothing chain | use-case |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `chart-line-dema-cross-pct` (11.874) | 2*EMA1 - EMA2 | lag-corrected dual smoothing |
| `chart-line-tema-cross-pct` (11.872) | 3*EMA1 - 3*EMA2 + EMA3 | triple smoothing |

DEMA cancels lag once; TEMA cancels twice. Both are scaled to
the close to make the magnitude comparable across instruments.
