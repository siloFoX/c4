## Summary

Added `<ChartLineHmaCrossPct />` -- a pure-SVG dual-panel
React/TS primitive that overlays the close with the Hull Moving
Average (HMA) in the top panel and renders the `close - HMA`
deviation scaled to the close as a percent in the bottom panel.
Different from `chart-line-hma-pct` (11.860) which divides the
deviation by the HMA value, this primitive divides by the close,
so the magnitude is comparable across instruments at different
price levels:

```
wma1[i]   = WMA(close, floor(length / 2))
wma2[i]   = WMA(close, length)
raw[i]    = 2 * wma1[i] - wma2[i]
HMA[i]    = WMA(raw, floor(sqrt(length)))
hmaPct[i] = close[i] === 0 ? null
                           : (close[i] - HMA[i]) / close[i] * 100
```

Regime classifier: `above` (hmaPct > 0), `below` (< 0),
`at` (= 0), `none` (null). Defaults: `length = 9` (canonical Hull
value).

## Bit-exact anchor

- **CONST close = K (K > 0)**: every WMA in the chain collapses to
  K via the `min === max` window-constant precision fix in
  `applyLineHmaCrossPctWma`. `raw = 2K - K = K`, `HMA = K`,
  `(close - HMA) / close * 100 = 0 / K * 100 = 0`. Regime `at`
  everywhere after warmup. Verified across multiple K and length
  tuples in the integration block. K = 0 triggers the divide-by-
  zero guard -> hmaPct = null.

## Difference vs chart-line-hma-pct (11.860)

| primitive | denominator | use-case |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `chart-line-hma-pct` (11.860) | HMA | percent of trend |
| `chart-line-hma-cross-pct` (11.873) | close | percent of price |

The cross-pct variant is comparable across instruments at very
different price magnitudes because the denominator is the price
itself, not the smoothed trend.
