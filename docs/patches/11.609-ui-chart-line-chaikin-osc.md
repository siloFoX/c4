## Summary

New `ChartLineChaikinOsc` UI primitive at
`web/src/components/ui/chart-line-chaikin-osc.tsx` -- a pure-SVG line
chart with a Chaikin Oscillator panel measuring
accumulation-distribution momentum. The top panel renders the close
price line; the bottom panel renders the oscillator line on a
symmetric y-axis with a dashed zero centerline and sign-coloured
markers.

## Indicator math

Marc Chaikin's Oscillator is the MACD of the Accumulation/Distribution
Line, with a fast period (default 3) and a slow period (default 10):

```
MFM[i]  = ((close - low) - (high - close)) / (high - low)
MFV[i]  = MFM[i] * volume[i]
ADL[i]  = ADL[i-1] + MFV[i]
ChaikinOsc = EMA(ADL, fastPeriod) - EMA(ADL, slowPeriod)
```

`computeLineChaikinOscMfm` is the Money Flow Multiplier;
`computeLineChaikinOscAdl` scales it by volume and accumulates the
running ADL; `computeLineChaikinOscEma` is an SMA-seeded EMA. The
oscillator -- the fast EMA minus the slow EMA of the ADL -- swings
around zero: positive when accumulation momentum is building,
negative when distribution momentum dominates.

### Worked fixture

`high = 10`, `low = 0` throughout; `close` at high (bars 0-3) then low
(bars 4-5); `volume = [4,4,4,4,8,8]`; `fast = 1`, `slow = 3`:

```
adl        = [4, 8, 12, 16, 8, 0]
fastEma    = EMA(adl, 1) = the ADL itself
slowEma    = EMA(adl, 3) = [.,.,8,12,10,5]
oscillator = fastEma - slowEma = [.,.,4,4,-2,-5]
```

Two readings above zero (accumulation momentum) and two below
(distribution momentum).

## API

- `getLineChaikinOscFinitePoints(points)` -- drop points with a
  non-finite `x`, `high`, `low`, `close` or `volume`.
- `normalizeLineChaikinOscPeriod(period, fallback)` -- positive
  integer; non-finite / sub-1 falls back, fractional floors.
- `computeLineChaikinOscMfm(highs, lows, closes)` -- the Money Flow
  Multiplier series.
- `computeLineChaikinOscAdl(highs, lows, closes, volumes)` -- returns
  `{ mfm, mfv, adl }`.
- `computeLineChaikinOscEma(src, period)` -- an SMA-seeded EMA.
- `computeLineChaikinOsc(highs, lows, closes, volumes, fastPeriod,
  slowPeriod)` -- returns `{ adl, fastEma, slowEma, oscillator }`.
- `runLineChaikinOsc(points, options?)` -- sort finite points by `x`,
  compute the ADL and oscillator, return `{ series, fastPeriod,
  slowPeriod, adl, fastEma, slowEma, oscillator, samples, oscFinal,
  oscMin, oscMax, positiveCount, negativeCount, ok }`. Each sample
  carries a `positive` / `negative` / `zero` sign. `ok` is false for
  fewer than two points.
- `computeLineChaikinOscLayout(options)` -- stack a price panel above
  an oscillator panel with a symmetric y-bound. Returns panels,
  ticks, the price path, the oscillator path, sign-coloured markers,
  and the zero centerline `y`.
- `describeLineChaikinOscChart(data, options?)` -- ARIA description.

## Component

`ChartLineChaikinOsc` is a `forwardRef` region (`role="region"`) with
an ARIA-linked description, a `role="img"` SVG, panel labels, a
config badge (EMA periods), a two-series legend (Price / Chaikin)
with toggle, a dashed zero centerline, the oscillator line, markers
coloured by sign, a hover/focus tooltip exposing the close, ADL,
oscillator, and sign, and a `motion-safe:animate-fade-in` entrance.
Every node carries a `data-section` attribute.

## Distinctness

- **vs chart-line-adl** -- the ADL primitive plots the *cumulative*
  Accumulation/Distribution Line itself. The Chaikin Oscillator takes
  that ADL as its *input* and plots the difference of two EMAs of it,
  so it measures the *momentum* of money flow rather than its running
  total.
- **vs chart-line-macd** -- MACD is the difference of two EMAs of the
  *price*. The Chaikin Oscillator is the difference of two EMAs of
  the *ADL* -- the same EMA-difference shape, but applied to money
  flow rather than price.
- **vs chart-line-mfi / chart-line-obv** -- those are bounded or
  cumulative volume measures. The Chaikin Oscillator is an unbounded
  momentum oscillator centred on zero.
