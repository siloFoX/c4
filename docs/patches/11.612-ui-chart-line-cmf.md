## Summary

New `ChartLineCmf` UI primitive at
`web/src/components/ui/chart-line-cmf.tsx` -- a pure-SVG line chart
with a Chaikin Money Flow panel. The top panel renders the close
price line; the bottom panel renders the CMF line on a fixed -1 to
+1 y-axis with a dashed zero centerline and markers coloured by
money-flow pressure.

## Indicator math

Marc Chaikin's Money Flow:

```
MFM[i] = ((close - low) - (high - close)) / (high - low)
MFV[i] = MFM[i] * volume[i]
CMF[i] = sum(MFV[i-period+1 .. i]) / sum(volume[i-period+1 .. i])
```

The Money Flow Multiplier `MFM` measures where the close sits within
the bar's high-low range -- a close at the high reads +1, at the low
-1, at the midpoint 0; a zero-range bar reads 0. Scaling it by volume
gives the Money Flow Volume. The CMF is the windowed sum of money
flow volume divided by the windowed sum of volume -- algebraically a
**volume-weighted average of the multiplier**, so the result is
itself bounded -1 to +1. CMF is defined from index `period - 1`
onward (a `period - 1` bar warm-up). A window with no volume reads 0
rather than dividing by zero.

### Worked fixture

Every bar has high 12, low 4 (range 8) so the multiplier is exact,
and any three consecutive volumes sum to 400:

```
close  = [12, 4, 10, 6, 12]   volume = [100, 100, 200, 100, 100]
mfm    = [1, -1, 0.5, -0.5, 1]
mfv    = mfm * volume = [100, -100, 100, -50, 100]
period = 3
cmf[2] = (100 - 100 + 100) / 400 =  0.25
cmf[3] = (-100 + 100 - 50) / 400 = -0.125
cmf[4] = (100 - 50 + 100) / 400  =  0.375
cmf    = [null, null, 0.25, -0.125, 0.375]
```

`cmfFinal = 0.375`, two buying periods, one selling period.

## API

- `getLineCmfFinitePoints(points)` -- drop points with a non-finite
  `x`, `high`, `low`, `close` or `volume`.
- `normalizeLineCmfPeriod(period, fallback)` -- coerce to a positive
  integer; non-finite or sub-1 falls back, fractional floors.
- `computeLineCmfMfm(highs, lows, closes)` -- the money flow
  multiplier series.
- `computeLineCmf(highs, lows, closes, volumes, period)` -- returns
  `{ mfm, mfv, cmf }`; `cmf` is null through the warm-up.
- `runLineCmf(points, options?)` -- sort finite points by `x`,
  compute the series, return `{ series, period, mfm, mfv, cmf,
  samples, cmfFinal, buyingCount, sellingCount, ok }`. Each sample
  carries a `buying` / `selling` / `flat` pressure. `ok` is false for
  fewer than two points.
- `computeLineCmfLayout(options)` -- stack a price panel above a CMF
  panel on a fixed -1 to +1 scale. Returns panels, ticks, the price
  path, the CMF path, pressure-coloured markers, and the centred
  zero line `y`.
- `describeLineCmfChart(data, options?)` -- ARIA description.

## Component

`ChartLineCmf` is a `forwardRef` region (`role="region"`) with an
ARIA-linked description, a `role="img"` SVG, panel labels, a config
badge, a two-series legend (Price / CMF) with toggle, a dashed zero
centerline, the CMF line, markers coloured by pressure (buying
green, selling red), a hover/focus tooltip exposing the high, low,
close, volume, multiplier, CMF, and pressure, and a
`motion-safe:animate-fade-in` entrance. Every node carries a
`data-section` attribute.

## Distinctness

- **vs chart-line-adl** -- the ADL is the *unbounded running
  cumulative total* of money flow volume from the first bar. The CMF
  *normalizes a windowed sum* of money flow volume by the windowed
  sum of volume, producing a bounded -1 to +1 oscillator with a
  `period - 1` bar warm-up. They share the multiplier formula but
  differ in accumulation: cumulative-total vs windowed-ratio.
- **vs chart-line-mfi** -- the Money Flow Index works from the
  *typical price* `(high + low + close) / 3` and a RSI-style ratio
  of positive to negative raw money flow on a 0 to 100 scale. The
  CMF weights volume by the *close's position in the range* and
  divides by raw volume on a -1 to +1 scale.
- **vs chart-line-chaikin-osc** -- the Chaikin Oscillator is the
  difference of two EMAs of the ADL (a MACD of the cumulative line).
  The CMF is a single windowed ratio, not an EMA difference.
