## Summary

New `ChartLineStdDev` UI primitive at
`web/src/components/ui/chart-line-stddev.tsx` -- a pure-SVG line
chart with a rolling standard deviation band overlay around a
moving average.

## Indicator math

The band has three lines around the price.

**Basis.** `computeLineStdDevBasis` is the `period`-bar simple
moving average.

**Standard deviation.** `computeLineStdDev` is the rolling
*population* standard deviation over the same window:

```
mean     = sum(window) / period
variance = sum((value - mean)^2) / period
stddev   = sqrt(variance)
```

**Bands.** `upper = basis + multiplier*stddev` and
`lower = basis - multiplier*stddev`. The band widens when the
price turns volatile and tightens when it settles -- a
Bollinger-style volatility channel. All three series are null
through the `period - 1` warm-up.

### Worked fixture

`period 2`, `multiplier 2`, price series `[10, 14, 11, 17, 12]`.
With a two-bar window the basis is the midpoint and the standard
deviation is exactly half the absolute bar-to-bar move, so every
value lands on a clean number:

```
[10,14] -> basis 12,   stddev 2,   upper 16,   lower 8
[14,11] -> basis 12.5, stddev 1.5, upper 15.5, lower 9.5
[11,17] -> basis 14,   stddev 3,   upper 20,   lower 8
[17,12] -> basis 14.5, stddev 2.5, upper 19.5, lower 9.5
```

The price runs above the basis on 2 bars and below on 2.

## API

- `getLineStdDevFinitePoints(points)` -- drop points with a
  non-finite `x` or `value`.
- `normalizeLineStdDevPeriod(period, fallback)` -- coerce to a
  positive integer.
- `normalizeLineStdDevMultiplier(multiplier, fallback)` --
  coerce to a positive finite number.
- `computeLineStdDevBasis(values, period)` -- the moving-average
  basis, null through the warm-up.
- `computeLineStdDev(values, period)` -- the rolling population
  standard deviation, null through the warm-up.
- `runLineStdDev(points, options?)` -- sort finite points by
  `x`, compute the basis, standard deviation and band series,
  return `{ series, period, multiplier, basis, stddev, upper,
  lower, samples, basisFinal, upperFinal, lowerFinal,
  aboveCount, belowCount, ok }`. Each sample is classified
  `above` / `below` / `on` the basis. `ok` is false for fewer
  than two points.
- `computeLineStdDevLayout(options)` -- project the price,
  basis, upper and lower paths plus a filled band area.
- `describeLineStdDevChart(data, options?)` -- ARIA
  description.

## Component

`ChartLineStdDev` is a `forwardRef` region (`role="region"`)
with an ARIA-linked description, a `role="img"` SVG, axis ticks
and grid, a config badge showing the period and multiplier, a
three-series legend (Price / Basis / Std dev band) with toggle,
a hover/focus tooltip exposing the value, basis, upper, lower
and position, and a `motion-safe:animate-fade-in` entrance. It
consumes `{ x, value }` points. The band edges are drawn as
upper and lower lines with a translucent filled area between
them; the basis is a separate line with per-bar markers. Every
node carries a `data-section` attribute.

## Distinctness

- **vs the smoothing overlays (chart-line-smma /
  chart-line-supersmoother)** -- those draw a single smoothed
  line. This primitive draws a volatility *channel*: a basis
  line bracketed by an upper and a lower band edge.
- **vs chart-line-roofing** -- the Roofing filter is a band-pass
  in the frequency sense. This band is a price channel whose
  width is the rolling dispersion of the price.
- **vs chart-line-disparity** -- disparity measures the percent
  gap from a moving average. This primitive measures the
  absolute dispersion and draws it as a band.
