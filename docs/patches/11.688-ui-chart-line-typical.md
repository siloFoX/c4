## Summary

New `ChartLineTypical` UI primitive in
`web/src/components/ui/chart-line-typical.tsx` -- a pure-SVG single-panel
line chart with a **Typical Price** overlay averaging the bar high, low and
close. The close line and the Typical Price line share one panel.

## The computation

The Typical Price is the simple average of each bar's high, low and close --
`(high + low + close) / 3` -- a smoothed proxy for the bar's "fair" level
that feeds many volume-weighted and band indicators. It works on an OHLC-like
bar, so `ChartLineTypicalPoint` carries `{x, high, low, close}`.

- `computeLineTypical(bars)` -- the Typical Price per bar. It is purely
  per-bar with no lookback, so it is defined for every bar; a bar with a
  non-finite high, low or close yields null.

Each bar is classified by where the Typical Price sits relative to the
close: **above** (the close finished in the lower part of its range),
**below** (the close finished strong, in the upper part) or **equal**.

## Worked fixture

The Typical Price is `(high + low + close) / 3`, an exact integer whenever
the three values sum to a multiple of 3. The fixture uses 8 OHLC bars chosen
so every `high + low + close` is divisible by 3:

```
bar      high low close  typical  zone
1         12   6    9       9     equal
2         15   9   15      13     below
3         18  12   12      14     above
4         21  15   18      18     equal
5         24  12   24      20     below
6         27  15   15      19     above
7         30  21   27      26     below
8         33  18   21      24     above
```

The Typical Price series `[9,13,14,18,20,19,26,24]` is asserted with
`toEqual`. The close sits level with the Typical Price (bars 1, 4), below it
(bars 3, 6, 8) and above it (bars 2, 5, 7), so all three zones are exercised:
3 above, 3 below, 2 equal; final Typical Price 24, final close 21.

## API

`data` (a `ChartLineTypicalPoint[]`), sizing/format/a11y props, `strokeWidth`
/ `dotRadius`, the colour props, the toggle flags (`showAxis` / `showGrid` /
`showDots` default false / `showTypical` / `showMarkers` / `showTooltip` /
`showConfigBadge` / `showLegend` / `animate`), controlled + uncontrolled
`hiddenSeries` (keys `close` / `typical`), `formatValue` / `formatX`,
`onPointClick` / `onSeriesToggle`. The Typical Price is parameter-free, so
there is no period prop.

Exported helpers: `getLineTypicalFinitePoints`, `computeLineTypical`,
`runLineTypical`, `computeLineTypicalLayout`, `describeLineTypicalChart`,
plus the `DEFAULT_CHART_LINE_TYPICAL_*` constants and the
`ChartLineTypicalZone` type. Every exported name carries a `LineTypical`
infix so the `export *` barrel line cannot collide.

## Accessibility

Root `<div role="region">` with `aria-label` + `aria-describedby`; SVG
`role="img"`; an off-screen `data-section="chart-line-typical-aria-desc"`
description span; the close line, the Typical Price line and every dot /
marker are `role="graphics-symbol" tabIndex={0}`. Every node carries a
`data-section`; the root mirrors `data-total-points`, `data-typical-final`,
`data-close-final`, `data-above-count`, `data-below-count`,
`data-equal-count` and `data-animate`.
